How to Refill a Prescription and Pay Your Utility Bill Online

This guide explains, step by step, how residents of San Ramon can refill a prescription at the community pharmacy and pay a monthly utility bill without visiting an office in person. These services are available around the clock and are designed to be usable from a phone, tablet, or computer.

Refilling a prescription

To refill a prescription, you will need the prescription number printed on the side of your medicine bottle. Enter that number, confirm your date of birth, and choose whether you want to pick the medication up or have it delivered. Most refills are ready within four hours. If the pharmacist needs to contact your doctor for a renewal, it may take one business day.

If you take several medications, you can enroll in synchronized refills so that all of your prescriptions are ready on the same day each month. This reduces the number of trips you need to make and makes it easier to keep track of what you have taken.

Paying a utility bill

To pay a utility bill, sign in with your account number and the last name on the account. You can pay a one-time amount, or set up automatic monthly payments from a bank account or card. A receipt is emailed to you immediately, and your payment history is stored so you can review past bills at any time.

If your bill is higher than usual, you can request a review or apply for the low-income assistance program directly from the same page. Payment plans are available for households that need to spread a large balance over several months.

Getting help

If you get stuck at any point, call the resident help line at the number listed on your bill, or visit the community center where a staff member can walk you through the process. Large-print instructions and translated versions are available on request.
