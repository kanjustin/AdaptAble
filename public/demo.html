Product description

Checking your blood pressure at home should not require reading a manual or squinting at a tiny screen. The ShopEasy Automatic Blood Pressure Monitor was designed with older adults and people with low vision in mind: a single large button starts the reading, and the results appear in oversized, backlit digits that are easy to see in any light.

The monitor inflates gently and gives a clear voice announcement of each result, so you do not have to rely on the screen alone. Readings are stored automatically, and a simple color indicator shows whether a result falls in a normal or elevated range — with the actual numbers always shown as well, so meaning never depends on color alone.

Everything in the box is labeled in large print, and the included guide walks through setup in five short steps. If you get stuck, our support team can walk you through it over the phone.
